About Us

We are Group 4. Our group is known for being cheerful, having a good sense of humor, and staying focused on our studies. We enjoy learning together and helping one another grow. We believe that being happy and positive makes learning more meaningful and enjoyable.

We organize tasks, guide the workflow, and keep the whole project on track—making sure everyone works smoothly and efficiently.

Discussion Wrap-Ups & Insights (Concluder)
